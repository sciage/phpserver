Candid is a private community dedicated towards giving platform of expressing your true feelings to the world. Other Social media platforms are open to your friends and your family members, and you can't express "How you are feeling about something?". Our private community provides security and protections towards your personal identity and let you be "Who you really want to be."

Information Collected and Received

Installation and Use

When you install and use Candid App, then Candid will collect basic personal information from social media websites for security reasons. This is a security measure to verify that only a real human being is registering with our network and preventing any artificial robot to gain access inside the app. This information may include the following: Person's name, age, gender, email address, personal contact number, phone book contacts, version of Candid app usage, and other information based on your interaction with our services. Candid may use the information collected the user for search results based on phone contacts, develop new features in our app, improve existing features, and to protect Candid and its users. Candid provides secure network for sharing anonymous posts which are relevant to the users.

Below are the details related to Permissions regarding the usage with the installation on Android platform.

User Profile

When a User registers a user profile, then they are redirected to their preferred social media platform to authenticate. The information taken about the user is governed by the "terms and conditions" of these Social Media platforms. Candid receives this information, store it inside secure Amazon RDS database, until the user intentional changes or deletes such data or terminates Candid Services. Compulsory information taken from the social media profile page includes Name, gender, email address, user ID. Collection and use of demographic information that is publicly available and additional contact information as allowed by applicable national laws.  

Please note that any content uploaded to and displayed in the user profile by the User is not moderated or altered by Candid. The User is solely liable for the publication of such content. Candid will store and process this information as long as you use our service will not use this information for any other purpose than required in order to obtain interoperability and functionality in relation to such third party services. Please note that we never store any passwords to the mentioned third party services.

Record Audio

Our Candid app provides 2 different ways of status posting. One is a text based format and second is audio based format. If the user chooses to provide audio based format, then they can record their own audio using the Android device microphone, and post their voice status in the community. We donot alter any audio recorded by the user. The user is solely responsible to the audio recording that he/she posts inside our anonymous community, and we donot support it. If any user gets hurt from these audio recordings, then they can contact us directly using Report Abuse section, and this harmful post will be removed immediately.

Contact Information

As the user provides basic information from social media providers, they get an additional service option, which may require the user to provide their personal contact information. You may share the  numbers and email addresses contained in Your device’s address book (“Contact Information”) with Candid for the purpose described below under Enhanced Search. If you provide us with personal information about someone else, you confirm that they are aware that You have provided their data and that they consent to our processing of their data according to our Privacy Policy.

Where the Candid Apps are obtained from Apple App Store or Google Play, we will only use permitted Contact Information to make our Services more relevant to You and Your engagement with the Candid community.

Please note that no other Contact Information other than the phone numbers like names and email addresses will be received, stored and used from the User’s address book. Other numbers or information that may be contained in your device’s phone book will be filtered away and will therefore not be stored or used by Candid. Please also note that You can always choose not to share Contact Information with Candid and if You have shared information and changed Your mind, You can easily delist Your number or opt-out to render your entire Contact Information unavailable for search in the database for Enhanced Search.
